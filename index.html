About Me

Hey, I'm Avery Ember Day — an illustrator and graphic designer based in Las Vegas. My work spans digital illustration, brand identity, and motion graphics, always with a focus on character, mood, and intentional design.

Six years as a barista taught me patience, attention to detail, and how to actually listen to what someone needs. Those same things drive how I approach design.

Outside of work I'm painting, sewing, traveling, and building the perfect playlist.
